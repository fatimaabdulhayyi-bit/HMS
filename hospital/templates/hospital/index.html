Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions patients ask about our hospital and services.

What features are available on the website and how can I access them?

Our website provides several healthcare features designed to make patient management easy and efficient. These include online appointment booking with doctors, secure medical record management (such as history, prescriptions, and reports), a real-time token tracking system to reduce waiting time, and an AI-based doctor finder feature that helps users quickly identify suitable doctors based on their needs. To access these features, you simply need to log in to your account. After login, you will be directed to your dashboard where all available services are clearly organized. From there, you can select and use any feature with just a few clicks.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by logging into your account and selecting the “Appointment Booking” option from the dashboard. After that, choose your preferred doctor, view available time slots, and confirm your booking. Once confirmed, your appointment details will be saved and you can view them anytime from your account.

How does the token system work?

The token system automatically generates a unique number for each patient when they check in or confirm an appointment. This token is used to manage and organize the patient queue in a systematic order, ensuring smooth workflow at the clinic or hospital. Patients can view their live token status through the dashboard, which shows their current position in the queue and helps estimate waiting time. This reduces crowding and improves overall efficiency in patient management.

who can access patient's medical record

Only authorized users can access a patient’s medical records. This includes the patient themselves and their assigned healthcare providers, such as doctors involved in their treatment. Access is strictly controlled to ensure that all medical information remains private, secure, and confidential at all times.

How do doctor recommendations work?

Our system uses patient needs and selected symptoms or medical concerns to suggest suitable doctors. Based on this information, the platform provides a list of relevant specialists to help users quickly find the most appropriate doctor for their condition. This makes it easier for patients to choose the right healthcare provider without confusion.
