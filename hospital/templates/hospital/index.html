Cardiology

Advanced heart care with modern diagnostic and treatment facilities.

Our Cardiology department provides complete heart care services including ECG, echocardiography, angiography, angioplasty, and bypass surgery. We manage conditions such as hypertension, arrhythmia, heart failure, and congenital heart diseases with expert cardiologists and modern technology.

Neurology

Specialized care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders.

The Neurology department treats a wide range of neurological conditions including stroke, epilepsy, migraine, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson’s disease, and neuropathies. With advanced neuroimaging, EEG, and rehabilitation services, we ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment plans for patients.

Dentistry

Providing comprehensive dental care with modern techniques and advanced equipment.

Our dentistry department offers preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dental services. From regular check-ups and cleaning to advanced procedures like root canal treatment, implants, and orthodontics, we ensure complete oral health care.

Dermatology

Expert skin, hair, and nail care for patients of all ages.

Our dermatology department provides treatments for common and complex skin disorders, cosmetic dermatology, acne care, skin allergies, and laser-based therapies. We combine expertise with technology for the best results.

Orthopaedic Surgery

Specialized care for bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments.

Our orthopaedic department deals with fractures, joint replacement, spine surgery, sports injuries, and rehabilitation. With modern surgical techniques and physiotherapy support, we ensure faster recovery and better mobility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions patients ask about our hospital and services.

How can I book an appointment with a doctor?

You can book an appointment online through our website, by calling our helpline, or directly at the hospital reception.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes, our emergency department is open 24/7 and fully equipped to handle urgent medical cases anytime.

Which medical insurance panels are accepted?

We accept most leading health insurance providers. Please check with our billing desk for confirmation regarding your insurance.

Can I consult doctors online?

Yes, we offer online consultation services for selected specialties. You can schedule them through our online portal.

How can I get my medical test reports?

Reports can be collected from the hospital laboratory counter or downloaded online using your patient ID.
